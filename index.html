ABOUT US

ExamPRESS helps students to prepare academically as well as mentally for exams. We not only provide practice test series but also allow students to have real time experience of an online exam. Our major motive is to alleviate the exam pressure the students undergo which is often overlooked by the educators and the parents. We provide a deeper understanding of the mistakes made by the students in the acadamics side and by their elders in understanding the psychology of the youth.

OUR TEST SERIES

Firstly we provide a practice test series wherein the students can solve practice questions and analyse their performance. Based on the analysis of students' performance in the practice series we provide personalized tutoring to students to help them focus on their weaknesses and prepare accordingly for the actual exam. After the actual exam, the performances of both the exams are compared and analysed after which we recommend our counsellors to the needful students.
